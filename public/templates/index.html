Keep only what matters to you

While multipurposes bots have a great chance to fit your needs, they also have a great chance to have features you don't need nor want. Felix was designed with that in mind and lets you disable commands/entire categories with the disable command, effectively making Felix ignore users who tries to use the disabled commands and hide them from the help command so your members know what they can use and what they can't.

Pin your best memes

Felix comes packed with a starboard feature, set it up with the starboard command, react with a star to the best messages out there and Felix will keep them in the starboard channel !

Power-up your server

Felix also brings along a bunch of features to make your server management easier: greetings, farewell, self-assignable roles, roles to automatically add to new members...

Keep the bad guys out

What kind of fool comes with server management features and not moderation ones? Not Felix ! You can set a mod-log channel to log (or not), ban, mute, softban, kick... All that with audit-log integration meaning Felix can also grab the actions and log it even if you don't use the commands

Throw your weebeeness at your friends

Felix not only comes with anime and manga commands to search up for mangas/animes, but also with the awesome weeb.sh image api to give you the best weeb images out there
